Gentoo penguin

Gentoos are the fastest underwater swimming penguins, reaching speeds of 36km/h (22mph).

  • Scientific Name: Pygoscelis papua
  • Average Length: 51 to 90 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 15-20 years
  • Habitat: Antarctic Peninsula

With flamboyant red-orange beaks, white-feather caps, and peach-colored feet, gentoo penguins stand out against their drab, rock-strewn Antarctic habitat.
